What we do

A vital link in the export supply chain

By integrating sustainable silviculture with industrial production, we manage thriving timberlands that supply the raw material for our export-grade wood products, ensuring shipments reach their destination intact.

01 / Compliance

ISPM 15 Heat Treatment

Every export-grade unit is heat-treated to a core temperature of at least 56°C for 30 minutes, meeting international phytosanitary standards and preventing delays at destination ports.

56°C · 30 MIN CORE
02 / Regeneration

Active Reforestation

For every tree harvested, we promote the planting of multiple seedlings, maintaining a continuous cycle of growth that sequesters carbon and protects local soil quality.

Our forests, our legacy

Sustainability isn't a slogan here

Since 2013, we've run a closed-loop forestry model that ensures production never outpaces nature's ability to regenerate.

Responsible Sourcing

Our wood comes from farms and forests managed for biodiversity and long-term ecosystem health.

Active Reforestation

For every tree harvested, multiple seedlings are planted, sequestering carbon and protecting local soil.

Circular Economy

Damaged pallets are refurbished for reuse; non-serviceable wood is recycled into mulch or biofuel.
