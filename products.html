Export-Grade Pallets

Our core product: heat-treated, ISPM 15 compliant pallets built for the realities of sea and air freight, from the Kenyan standard to EPAL-grade Euro pallets.

  • Kenyan Standard1200 × 1000mm
  • Euro Pallet (EPAL-grade)1200 × 800mm
  • Safe Working LoadUp to 1,200kg
  • MaterialsEucalyptus / Cypress / Pine
  • TreatmentISPM 15 · 56°C / 30 min core

Angle Board Corners

Reinforcement strips that protect pallet edges and cargo corners during transit, sized specifically for sea versus air freight.

  • Sea freight length2400mm
  • Air freight length1600mm / 1000mm
  • FunctionEdge & corner reinforcement
  • MaterialsEucalyptus / Pine

Pallet Tops

Flat, heat-treated deck caps used to close off the top of a stacked or palletized load for sea freight, keeping cargo compressed and protected under load-securing straps.

  • Kenyan Standard top1200 × 1000mm
  • Euro top (EPAL-grade)1200 × 800mm
  • Typical useSea freight, stacked loads
  • TreatmentISPM 15 on request

Custom Dimensions

Where a client's cargo doesn't fit a standard footprint, our production team builds to the exact dimensions the shipment requires.
