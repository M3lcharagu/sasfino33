Common questions

Before you reach out

A few things clients usually ask before requesting a quote.

How long does an order take, from quote to dispatch?+

Lead time depends on quantity, dimensions, and whether the order is a standard or custom build. Standard-size pallets typically move faster than custom builds, which require dimension sign-off before production starts. Exact timelines are discussed once we understand your order.

Do all products come with ISPM 15 heat treatment?+

Our export-grade pallets are heat-treated to ISPM 15 as standard, reaching a core temperature of 56°C for 30 minutes. Angle board corners, pallet tops, and custom builds can be heat-treated on request depending on the shipment's destination requirements.

Can you build to a non-standard size?+

Yes. Where a client's cargo doesn't fit the Kenyan standard or EPAL-grade Euro footprint, our production team builds to the exact dimensions the shipment requires. Mention this in your message and we'll follow up with next steps.

Which ports and freight types do you supply for?+

Our pallets and angle board corners move through the Port of Mombasa and Jomo Kenyatta International Airport on behalf of horticultural exporters, tea and coffee estates, and freight forwarders across East Africa, for both sea and air freight.
